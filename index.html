Who We Are

The LSU Water Brigades is a student organization dedicated to addressing water accessibility issues both locally and globally. We work to provide clean water solutions, raise awareness about water-related challenges, and engage in community service projects that make a lasting impact.

Our team consists of passionate LSU students from diverse academic backgrounds who share a common goal: ensuring that everyone has access to clean, safe water.

What We Do

Our comprehensive approach to water accessibility and community service

Environmental Conservation

We organize lake cleanups and environmental restoration projects to protect local water sources and ecosystems in the Baton Rouge area.

Community Building

Partnering with organizations like Habitat for Humanity to build sustainable housing with proper water infrastructure.

Water Quality Advocacy

Working with the Stormwater Coalition to improve water quality and management in our local communities.

Brigade Missions

Organizing and participating in water brigade missions to provide direct support and resources to communities in need.
