For LSU Students

Join a community of passionate students making a real impact

Why Join LSU Water Brigades?

  • Make Real Impact: Work on projects that directly improve communities and water access
  • Develop Skills: Gain experience in project management, community engagement, and advocacy
  • Build Networks: Connect with like-minded students and community partners
  • Leadership Opportunities: Take on leadership roles and develop professional skills
  • Service Learning: Apply classroom knowledge to real-world challenges
  • Personal Growth: Challenge yourself and discover your potential for social impact

What You'll Do

  • Participate in local environmental cleanup projects
  • Support water infrastructure development initiatives
  • Organize educational campaigns and awareness events
  • Collaborate with community partners on service projects
  • Attend training workshops and skill-building sessions
  • Help plan and execute water brigade missions
  • Advocate for water justice and policy changes
  • Document and share stories of community impact

Join as a Volunteer

Ready to join our team? Here's how to get started with LSU Water Brigades!

What to Include in Your Email

  • Your Name: First and last name
  • Contact Info: Email and phone number
  • Academic Year: Freshman, sophomore, junior, senior, or graduate
  • Major/Field of Study: Your area of study at LSU
  • Areas of Interest: Which projects interest you most?
  • Experience: Any previous volunteer experience
  • Motivation: Why you want to join our mission
  • Availability: Your general schedule and availability

Ways to Support Us
