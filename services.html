Our Services

Comprehensive programs for water accessibility and community development

What We Offer

Our comprehensive approach to addressing water accessibility challenges through multiple service areas

Community-Centered Approach

All of our services are designed with community needs at the center. We work closely with local partners to ensure our programs address real challenges and create lasting positive impact.

Our team of dedicated LSU students brings diverse skills and perspectives to every project, while always respecting local knowledge and cultural contexts.

Sustainable Solutions

We focus on creating long-term, sustainable solutions that communities can maintain and benefit from for years to come. Our programs emphasize education, capacity building, and environmental stewardship.

Every service we provide includes components for ongoing support and community empowerment.

Our Core Services

Environmental Conservation

Protecting and restoring local water sources through hands-on conservation efforts and environmental advocacy.

What We Do:

  • LSU Lakes cleanup and restoration projects
  • Water quality monitoring and testing
  • Habitat restoration along waterways
  • Community education on water conservation
  • Advocacy for environmental protection policies

Impact:

Our environmental conservation efforts help preserve local ecosystems, improve water quality, and create healthier communities for everyone.

Community Development

Building sustainable infrastructure and partnerships that ensure long-term water access and community resilience.

What We Do:

  • Partnership with Habitat for Humanity
  • Water infrastructure development support
  • Community capacity building workshops
  • Sustainable development planning
  • Technical assistance and training

Impact:

We help communities build the infrastructure and skills they need for long-term water security and sustainable development.

Stormwater Management

Working with the Stormwater Coalition to improve water quality and reduce flooding in our communities.

What We Do:

  • Stormwater system assessment and improvement
  • Rain garden installation and maintenance
  • Community workshops on stormwater management
  • Policy advocacy for better water management
  • Research on innovative stormwater solutions

Impact:

Our stormwater management efforts reduce flooding, improve water quality, and create more resilient communities.

Water Brigade Missions

Direct service missions that provide immediate assistance and long-term solutions for water access challenges.

What We Do:

  • Water system installation and repair
  • Emergency water relief distribution
  • Community needs assessment
  • Technical training for local teams
  • Long-term partnership development

Impact:

Our brigade missions provide both immediate relief and sustainable solutions that communities can maintain independently.

Educational Programs

Building awareness and capacity through education and advocacy

Campus Outreach

Educational campaigns and events that raise awareness about water issues among LSU students and faculty.

  • Water awareness week events
  • Guest speaker presentations
  • Educational booth campaigns
  • Collaborative campus initiatives

Community Workshops

Hands-on learning opportunities that teach practical skills for water conservation and management.

  • Water conservation techniques
  • Rainwater harvesting systems
  • Water quality testing methods
  • Emergency preparedness planning

Policy Advocacy

Research and advocacy efforts that promote policies supporting water justice and accessibility.

  • Policy research and analysis
  • Legislative advocacy campaigns
  • Coalition building with other organizations
  • Public awareness campaigns

Special Programs

Unique initiatives that address specific community needs

Volunteer Leadership Development

Comprehensive training programs that prepare students to become effective water advocates and community leaders.

Program Components:

  • Leadership skills workshops
  • Project management training
  • Cultural competency education
  • Technical skills development
  • Mentorship opportunities

Our leadership development program ensures that every volunteer has the skills and knowledge needed to make a meaningful impact in their work with LSU Water Brigades.

Emergency Response Support

Rapid response capabilities for water-related emergencies and disasters in our region and beyond.

Response Capabilities:

  • Emergency water distribution
  • Temporary water purification systems
  • Coordination with relief organizations
  • Community needs assessment
  • Long-term recovery planning

When disasters strike, our team is ready to provide immediate assistance while also planning for long-term recovery and resilience.
